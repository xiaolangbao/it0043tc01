Jollibee

Bee

Jollibee is a large red human-sized bee with red and yellow stripes. He has big black eyes, a red tuxedo with a black bowtie, a white chef hat, and large yellow shoes. Jollibees wings are clear white. Back in his earliest appearance in 1978, he had green wings and pants, and black shoes. His undershirt was also orange and he had thin antennae. Most notably, he had a sting, which was never seen again. After being redesigned by Willie Villano in the early 1980s, his head became a little wider, his body became a little slender, and he was colored a lighter shade of red, somewhat like red orange.

Mr.Yum

Burger

Yum is an average-sized boy with light skin, dark brown hair, and brown eyes. Since his redesign in 2008, he wears a hamburger-shaped baseball cap, yellow goggles, a purple shirt with blue and orange sleeves with his name in the center, a black bag in his waist, green cargo pants, and blue and white sneakers. The initial design character, back when he was called Mr. Yum, is portrayed as an adult postman with black eyes and hair. His burger hat was shaped like a fedora, and he wore a dark blue business suit and pants, a white undershirt, and a red necktie. Around the middle 1990s, his blue suit was recolored into light yellow, and a single blue suspender now holds his pants.

Hetty

Spaghetti

She has blonde pigtails that represents spaghetti and wears a pink and magenta cheerleader outfit, along with white socks and dark pink sneakers. Back in 1984, her hair originally had red bow (later red spots that resembles sauce) and a blue headband. She wore a pink overalls with her name on it and a white and blue puffed sleeves, white socks with pink and blue stripes on each side, and white shoes (later blue shoes)

Twirlie

Sundae

Her hair has a cream fringe and is tied in an updo that resembles a cone twirl, and has a silver headband with two stars. She wears a pink minidress with white swirls in the center, a belt with her name on it, a purple coat with lilac frills, dark purple leggings and pink boots with lighter pink frills. Her design back then has short hair, later long and tied in twintails. She wore a white cone twirl hat with her name on it, a white and red buttoned dress decorated with strawberries (later sundaes), white socks and red shoes with white soles and green knots.

Popo

French Fries

He has blonde hair which resembles french fries and he wears a blue tracksuit with orange and white stripe and an orange cap with blank blue on it, along with orange sneakers. His design in 1970 back then had a red hat with his name on it in yellow letters, and wore a red and white suit with black bow and black pants (Later he wore a red shirt and blue overalls with blue sneakers)
